Our Policies

Booking & Confirmation

  • 30% advance payment required for booking
  • Remaining 70% due 7 days before travel
  • Booking confirmation within 4 hours
  • E-ticket & itinerary sent via email/WhatsApp

Cancellation Policy

  • 30+ days before trip: 90% refund
  • 15-29 days before trip: 70% refund
  • 7-14 days before trip: 50% refund
  • Less than 7 days: No refund
  • Weather-related cancellations: 100% refund

Rescheduling Policy

  • Free reschedule up to 15 days before trip
  • 500 BDT fee for reschedule within 7-14 days
  • Reschedule allowed only once per booking
  • New date must be within 3 months

Safety & Responsibility

  • Travel insurance recommended for all trips
  • First aid kit available on all tours
  • Not responsible for lost personal items
  • Force majeure: alternate arrangements made

Child Policy

  • Under 2 years: Free (no seat)
  • 2-6 years: 50% of package price
  • 6-12 years: 75% of package price
  • Above 12 years: Full price

Payment Methods

  • bKash / Nagad / Rocket
  • Bank Transfer (all major banks)
  • Cash at our Dhaka office
  • International Card (Visa/Mastercard)

Important Terms

All prices are per person in BDT (Bangladeshi Taka) for standard 2-night/3-day packages unless specified otherwise. Group discounts available for 5+ persons. Custom itineraries can be arranged with flexible pricing. For any disputes, the laws of Bangladesh shall apply, and the jurisdiction lies in Dhaka, Bangladesh.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is included in the package?

Transport, accommodation, guide fees, and basic meals as per itinerary. Check individual package details.

Can I customize my trip?

Absolutely! Contact us with your preferences and we'll design a personalized itinerary for you.

Do you provide travel insurance?

We recommend purchasing travel insurance separately. We can assist with recommendations.

How do I confirm my booking?

Pay 30% advance via bKash/Nagad/bank transfer and send us the screenshot. You'll receive confirmation within 4 hours.

What if the weather is bad?

We monitor weather conditions. If unsafe, we reschedule or provide 100% refund.

Do you offer group discounts?

Yes! Groups of 5+ get 10% off, 10+ get 15% off, and 20+ get 20% off.
